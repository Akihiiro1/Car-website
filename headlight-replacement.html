Headlight Replacement

Proper headlight function is essential for safe driving. Modern vehicles use various types of headlights, including halogen, LED, and HID (High-Intensity Discharge) systems.

Headlights naturally dim over time and eventually burn out. Regular inspection and timely replacement ensure optimal visibility and safety.

Many vehicles use different bulbs for high beams, low beams, and daytime running lights. It's important to use the correct replacement bulb for each function.

Some modern vehicles have adaptive or automatic headlight systems that require special consideration during replacement.

Headlight Maintenance Guidelines

  • Check headlight function regularly
  • Replace bulbs in pairs
  • Use correct bulb type
  • Clean headlight lenses
  • Adjust aim if necessary

Benefits of Proper Headlight Maintenance

  • Ensures optimal visibility
  • Improves safety
  • Meets legal requirements
  • Prevents accidents
  • Enhances appearance
