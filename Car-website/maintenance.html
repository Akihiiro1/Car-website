Battery Replacement

Learn how to safely replace your vehicle's battery and extend its lifespan with proper maintenance.

  • Signs: Slow engine crank, dim headlights, electrical issues.
  • Steps: Disconnect old battery, clean terminals, install new battery, check voltage.
  • Benefits: Reliable starts, prevents breakdowns, protects electronics.

Oil Change

Step-by-step guide to changing your vehicle's oil and maintaining engine health for optimal performance.

  • Signs: Dirty oil, engine noise, oil warning light.
  • Steps: Drain old oil, replace filter, add new oil, check level.
  • Benefits: Smoother engine, better fuel economy, longer engine life.

Brake Service

Master the essential skills for inspecting and maintaining your vehicle's critical brake system components.

  • Signs: Squeaking, grinding, longer stopping distance.
  • Steps: Inspect pads/rotors, replace worn parts, bleed brakes, test drive.
  • Benefits: Safer driving, prevents costly repairs, peace of mind.

Tire Rotation

Proper technique for rotating your tires to ensure even wear and extend their lifespan.

  • Signs: Uneven tread wear, vibration, poor handling.
  • Steps: Move tires to new positions, check pressure, inspect for damage.
