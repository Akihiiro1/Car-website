Our Story

Car Care Guide is a startup focused on providing how-to videos for your car that are high-quality, concise and completely free. We believe that many people would take better care of their cars if only they knew how.

Our team of Holy Crossian Project creates professional how-to videos that show drivers exactly how to maintain and repair their vehicles. This helps users drive vehicles that last longer, perform better, and are more efficient.

Our community tells us that our videos empowered them to do things they were curious about but were too afraid or embarrassed to ask for help... more importantly, they tell us that they had fun fixing their car and that our videos saved them serious money.
