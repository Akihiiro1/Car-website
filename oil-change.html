Oil Change

Regular oil changes are crucial for maintaining your engine's health and performance. Engine oil serves multiple critical functions in your vehicle:

Oil is the lifeblood of your engine, providing essential lubrication to reduce friction between moving parts. As oil circulates through your engine, it also helps cool components and carries away harmful debris and contaminants.

Over time, engine oil breaks down and becomes contaminated with particles, reducing its effectiveness. This degradation can lead to increased engine wear and reduced performance if not addressed through regular oil changes.

Modern engines are designed to operate with specific oil types and weights. Using the correct oil specification is crucial for optimal performance and longevity.

Oil Change Best Practices

  • Follow your vehicle manufacturer's recommended oil change intervals
  • Use the specified oil grade and weight for your engine
  • Replace the oil filter with every oil change
  • Check oil levels regularly between changes
  • Keep records of oil changes and mileage

Benefits of Regular Oil Changes

  • Extends engine life
  • Improves fuel efficiency
  • Maintains engine performance
  • Prevents costly repairs
  • Reduces harmful emissions
