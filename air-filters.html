Air Filters

Air filters are crucial components that protect your engine from harmful debris and contaminants. Both the engine air filter and cabin air filter play important roles in your vehicle.

The engine air filter prevents dust, dirt, and debris from entering your engine. Clean air is essential for proper combustion and engine performance.

The cabin air filter helps maintain air quality inside your vehicle by filtering out pollen, dust, and other airborne particles. This is especially important for passengers with allergies or respiratory sensitivities.

Regular air filter replacement ensures proper airflow and filtration. Clogged filters can reduce performance and efficiency.

Air Filter Maintenance Guidelines

  • Check filters regularly for dirt and debris
  • Replace filters at recommended intervals
  • Use quality replacement filters
  • Consider environment when setting replacement schedule
  • Inspect for proper installation

Benefits of Clean Air Filters

  • Improves engine performance
  • Increases fuel efficiency
  • Enhances air quality
  • Protects engine components
  • Reduces emissions
